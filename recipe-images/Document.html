Lasagna recipe

Delicious lasagna for the whole family!

This lasagna requires some work, but it is worth is! We guarantee the whole family will love it

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta texture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
